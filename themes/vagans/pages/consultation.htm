Free Consultation

At Vagans Legal Services, it is our goal to support our partners and clients in the legal community in every way possible. Vagans Legal offer a wide variety of legal support services to provide a broader coverage of legal issues. Whether you are looking for a legal support regarding the small claims court, the Landlord and Tenant Board, Traffic Court or otherwise, Sriram Rangan - Licensed Paralegal will listen to your case and discuss your options with you confidentially.

When you reach Vagans Legal Services for a free consultation, Sriram Rangan - Licensed Paralegal will give a free consultation of up to 30 minutes to help you determine your rights and options. This session is non-binding and does not represent any form of retainer or paralegal – client relationship. It is aimed to gain an insight into the case and evaluate your legal support requirements. Only signing a retainer agreement will constitute a Paralegal client relationship.
